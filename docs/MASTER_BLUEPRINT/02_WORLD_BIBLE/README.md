# 02 — World Bible

## Purpose

To hold the canonical facts of the OSIRIS.EXE universe — its places, history,
rules, and metaphysics — so every artifact agrees on the same reality.

## Scope

The world itself: locations, factions, technology, mythology, symbols, and the
laws that govern them. Not the plot (see Story Bible) or the cast (see Character
Bible).

## Principles

- One canon. Contradictions are resolved here, dated, and explained.
- Real history is treated with accuracy and respect.
- Establish rules before exceptions; the world must feel consistent.

## Current Status

Template. Canonical material exists in the companion `osiris-motion-comic/canon/`
wiki and should be migrated/reconciled into structured entries here.

## Open Questions
