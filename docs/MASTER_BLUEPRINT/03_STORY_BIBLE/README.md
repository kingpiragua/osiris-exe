# 03 — Story Bible

## Purpose

To define the narrative of OSIRIS.EXE — its arcs, timeline, themes, and the
order in which memories are recovered.

## Scope

Plot, structure, the recovered-memory sequence, thematic throughlines, and
continuity. Not world facts (World Bible) or cast detail (Character Bible).

## Principles

- Story is **recovered**, not narrated at the viewer.
- Theme leads plot: memory, erasure, lineage, resistance.
- Every recovered memory must stand alone *and* serve the whole.

## Current Status

Template. Source narrative material exists in `osiris-motion-comic` (running
order, panels) and should be reconciled here as the recovered-memory canon.

## Open Questions

- Recovery order vs. chronological order of memories.
- How much story is told in-interface vs. inside recovered memories.
