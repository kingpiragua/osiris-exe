# 16 — Future Expansion

## Purpose

To hold ideas for what OSIRIS.EXE could become — beyond the current roadmap —
without committing to them.

## Scope

Speculative directions: new artifact types, formats, platforms, interactions, or
narrative surfaces. A holding ground for "someday," kept separate from the
committed Roadmap.

## Principles

- Capture freely; commit deliberately. Nothing here is promised.
- Every idea must still pass the Charter ("deepen recovering memory").
- Park ideas with enough context to evaluate later.

## Current Status

Template. Open for capture.

## Open Questions

- Which speculative directions align with the Charter vs. dilute it.
- Criteria for promoting an idea from here to the Roadmap.
