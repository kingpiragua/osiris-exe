# 14 — Marketing

## Purpose

To define how OSIRIS.EXE is positioned and shared — without betraying its tone.

## Scope

Positioning, audience, voice in external channels, launch strategy, and assets
(trailers, stills, copy). Distinct from in-world creative direction.

## Principles

- Marketing speaks in the world's voice; it does not break the spell.
- Show restraint and mystery — reveal less than asked.
- Authenticity over hype; the history is treated with respect.

## Current Status

Template. No active campaign. Companion `osiris-motion-comic` has a public
presence that may inform positioning.

## Open Questions

- Primary audience and channels.
- How to trailer a silence/recovery experience without spoiling it.
- Relationship/branding between `osiris-exe` and `osiris-motion-comic`.
